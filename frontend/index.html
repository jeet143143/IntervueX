          About the Platform

          IntervueX is an advanced AI-powered platform designed to help you prepare for technical interviews at top companies like Google, Amazon, Meta, Microsoft, and Apple.

          • 🤖 AI Mock Interviews — Realistic video-call style interviews with an AI avatar that speaks questions aloud
          • 🎤 Voice Answers — Speak your answers naturally using your microphone with real-time transcription
          • 💻 Code Editor — Built-in code notepad for coding questions with line numbers and language selection
          • 📊 Smart Evaluation — AI evaluates clarity, accuracy, and communication with detailed feedback
          • 📄 Resume Analyzer — Upload your PDF resume for ATS compatibility analysis and improvement tips
          • 📈 Dashboard — Track your progress with score trends and identify weak areas
          🚀

          How to Use

          1. Create an Account

            Sign up with your email to track your progress across sessions.

          2. Start a Mock Interview

            Select your target role, company, and difficulty level. Click "Begin Interview" to enter the video call room.

          3. Answer Questions

            The AI interviewer will ask questions aloud. Use your microphone to speak your answer, or open the code editor for coding questions.

          4. Get Instant Feedback

            After submitting, receive detailed scores on clarity, accuracy, and communication with improvement suggestions.

          5. Track Progress

            Visit the Dashboard to see your score trends and identify areas that need more practice.

          ❓

          Frequently Asked Questions

          Is this platform free?

          Yes! The platform is completely free to use with unlimited mock interviews.

          Do I need a camera and microphone?

          It's recommended for the best experience, but not required. You can still use the code editor to write answers.

          Which companies are supported?

          Google, Amazon, Meta, Microsoft, Apple, and a Generic option covering standard interview patterns.

          How does the AI evaluation work?

          Powered by LLaMA 3.3 70B via Groq, the AI evaluates your answers on clarity, technical accuracy, and communication skills.
